Chapter History



Real Community Chapter of Sigma
(Calamba City Laguna Triskelion)

The Tau Gamma Sigma Triskelion Grand Sorority of Real Chapter was organized by Former CCTCS Chairwoman Mayla Oliveros and Vice External May Redaja through the sponsorship of Sis Julie Ann Culabat (Hanggan 1 Chapter -BMC), Sis “Abeng” Joyce Audrey Culabat (Farcon Ville, Parian Chapter), Sis “Yeye” Jierelle Alcantara (Sto.Domingo Chapter -BMC), and Sis Jeanette Cometa (Bandola Chapter) dated January 17, 2009. The objective of organizing the chapter is to be part of community development as well as camaraderie among all members of our Sorority, with the help of Tau Gamma Phi Triskelion Grand Fraternity.

Presently, the Real Chapter continued as an active organization in the community, honoring our Tau Gamma Phi/Sigma, Grand Fraternity/ Sorority, Our Supreme Fraternity/ Sorority.
