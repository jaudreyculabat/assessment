Chapter Policy


Admission/ Recruitment

Pre-screening Requirement

1.1.1 All alexis/ medusa are selected based on their physical and mental fitness skills, exemplary moral character, and potential.
1.1.2 All alexis/ medusa are required to take one (1) week of service. Qualified alexis/ medusa will undergo interview processing. Those who will pass the interview will be advised to prepare for their Final Initiation Rites.
1.1.3 All alexis/ medusa are required to report all chapter members, for them to introduce their selves, as their service will go on.
1.1.4 All alexis/ medusa must be prohibited drug-free dependents and for all medusa include their pregnancy test result.

Final Initiation Rites

1.2.1 All officers and members are required to attend the final initiation rites of all alexis/ medusa who passed the pre-screening requirements.
1.2.2 The Master Wielder of Whip/ Lady Master Wielder of Whip will be given full authority during final initiation rites assisted by the GT/ GLT and DGT/ DGLT
1.2.3 All officers and members are not allowed to hold the paddle if proven under the influence of prohibited drugs, liquors, and the like.
1.2.4 Observance of the initiation stages shall be strictly enforced. 1.2.5 All recordable gadgets like cellphones, video cams, and camcorders are not allowed to be used during initiation rites.
1.2.6 Protect the Whip at all times.
1.2.7 The place of the initiation rites will depend on the availability of the place and agreed upon.
1.2.8 The GT/ GLT/ Officers must elaborate to alexis/ medusa on the scope of the waiver before their signatory.
1.2.9 alexis/ medusa should read and understand the waiver given to them before the initiation rites.
1.2.10 alexis/ medusa must wear a plain white shirt and cycling shorts.
1.2.11 During the initiation rites No drinking of alcoholic beverages. Any other sessions are NOT ALLOWED as they could harm both the chapter and alexis/ medusa. Respect the chapter.
1.2.12 During the initiation rites, any drunken members or associates who intake high substances like drugs and weeds are not allowed to attend the initiation rites as it may harm both the chapter members and the alexis/ medusa. Respect the chapter.
1.2.13 Visitors are NOT ALLOWED or AUTHORIZED to hold the paddle unless they have the consent of the "Master Wielder of the Whip/ Lady Master Wielder of the Whip".
1.2.14 The rules of hazing are in effect:
1.2.14.1 Physical Rules are in effect (Slapping, Candling, and Belting on hand and in the back) but these are subject to limitation.
1.2.15 The use of chili and ammonia during the initiation rites is in effect but depends on the situation therein. 1.2.16 Visitation during final:
1.2.16.1 House rules are in effect and apply to all the visitors during initiation rites
1.2.16.2 Permission should be asked from the chapter officers.
1.2.17 Visitors should provide their transportation or fare.
1.2.18 Violations of the above mention will be Disciplinary Action depending on the severity they've caused to the chapter members and alexis/ medusa.

First Offense: 3 Dynamic forces
Second Offense: 6 Tradition

During Initiation Rites for the member

1.5.1 All Tau Gamma Phi/ Sigma shall not wear the fraternity/ sorority shirt while in the initiation rites.
1.5.2 All members who will conduct the Initiation Rites will be placed in designated work to less and avoid casualties.
1.5.3 Members who will conduct the initiation rites must be a member for Six (6) months and above unless there is consent from the GT/ GLT and MI/ LMI.
1.5.4 Members who will conduct the initiation rites must be active members of their chapter (Real Chapter).
1.5.5 Those who are under suspension and inactive members are NOT ALLOWED to attend the said Initiation Rites. They must fulfill first the period of their suspension and be an active member of the chapter for approval.
1.5.6 No shouting or horse playing while conducting an Initiation Rites.
1.5.7 No taking of Pictures and Video during IR is prohibited unless as necessary.
1.5.8 Tau Gamma Phi / Brods are NOT ALLOWED to attend or touch the MEDUSA in any matter unless they need support during the said Initiation Rites. Brothers who will insist will be given Disciplinary action.
1.5.9 Initiation rites are given the full support of each member of the REAL CHAPTER OFFICERS.
1.5.10 Violations of the above mention will be Disciplinary Action depending on the severity they've caused to the chapter members and alexis/ medusa.

Orientation

2.1 Orientation is initiated to facilitate the induction of new members into the organization. The Grand Triskelion/ Grand Lady Triskelion will be given full authority in conducting orientation. Their primary duty is to give information to the new member about our Codes of Conduct, Tenets, Chapter Policy, Bylaws, Officers, and the Fraternity/ Sorority.
2.2 The Officers are responsible to give a briefing to all new members of the Fraternity/ Sorority, on their duties and responsibilities and their role as a member of the Real Chapter and the Fraternity/ Sorority.
2.3 New members of the chapter are under a (6) six-month probationary period.
2.4 No members are allowed to be welcome in other chapters unless they have already reached the six (6) months probationary period.
2.5 Brothers/ Sisters that are not born as a member of the chapter but vouch as a legit members of the TAU GAMMA PHI/ SIGMA TRISKELION GRAND FRATERNITY/ SORORITY, will need to pass COL for confirmation purposes before the welcoming in the chapter.
2.6 All the members therein in the chapter must comply with and follow the house rules.
2.7 Violations of the above mention will be Disciplinary Action depending on the severity they've caused to the chapter.

First Offense: 3 Dynamic forces
Second Offense: 6 Tradition

Election

3.1 Each candidate is allowed the opportunity to campaign and announce their candidacy.
3.2 The Candidate must be a 1-year member/welcome.
3.3 Candidate must be from Real Chapter, not necessarily an original member but must be officially welcomed and resident on Real, Calamba.
3.4 No candidate or currently seated official can inherit a position and any changes in availability or/ of wishes to change position/s must be held through an election.
3.5 All elected officers must contribute and perform their duties to the best that is required of such a position, they were elected on their position and must hold up the respect of the position they were elected to.
3.6 Repercussions of not being able to do their duties and responsibilities as an elected officer of Real Chapter.

Member's Responsibilities and Integrity

Meeting Agenda

4.1.1 Every bonafide member of the TAU GAMMA PHI/ SIGMA REAL CHAPTER must attend the designated time and date of the meeting place by the Grand Triskelion/ Grand Lady Triskelion.
4.1.2 A meeting is necessary and attendance is a must. It is the only way to gather all knowledge, ideas, and plans to come up with better projects, problems of the Real Chapter, violations of the members, or even personal problems can be open to the meeting as long as they will be a concern and harm the fraternity/ sorority.
4.1.3 Each of the bona fide members of TAU GAMMA PHI/ SIGMA REAL CHAPTER who fail to attend the said meeting without prior notice will be given a chance to explain themselves. Any reason they may have will be validated by the GT/ GLT and Chapter officers.
4.1.4 The GT/ GLT will justify the given reason and rely on the Officers for validation.
4.1.5 Smoking while in the meeting is prohibited.
4.1.6 The meeting should be held without any alcoholic beverages of any sort.
4.1.7 Members under the influence of alcohol are NOT ALLOWED to attend the said meeting.
4.1.8 Violations of the above mention will be Disciplinary Action depending on the severity they've caused to the chapter.

First Offense: 3 Dynamic forces
Second Offense: 6 Tradition

Absences

4.2.1 When absences from meetings and activities cannot be avoided, a member is required to immediately notify the GT/ GLT, DGT/ DLGT, or any other officer available

Transfer

4.3.1 The transfer involves movements of fellow Triskelion from one chapter to this chapter or vice versa.
4.3.2 Fellow Triskelion must give notice to his/her previous Chapter’s GT/ GLT and need confirmation from them (proof of approval) before the welcome.
4.3.3 Fellow Triskelion must provide Certificate of Legitimacy from root chapter.
4.3.4 Welcome six (6) all full swing shall be given for that Brother/ Sister Triskelion who wishes to be a member of this Chapter.

Safety

4.4.1 Safety is everybody's concern. Most accident involves both unsafe condition and unsafe human practices. To prevent an accident, no officers and members will be allowed to hold the Paddle or conduct any initiation rites under the influence of alcohol, prohibited drugs, and any similar nature.
4.4.2 First aid medicine and equipment must be provided and ready during activities, especially during Initiation Rites.

Funds

4.5.1 Any amount per regular meeting will be collected by the Master Keeper of the Chess/ Lady Master Keeper of the Chess to raise a fund for operational purposes and necessities of the chapter.
4.5.2 The MKC/ LMKC will be responsible for the collection and recording. They are advised to do a financial report at every meeting and inform the officers and members of this chapter of its financial status.
4.5.3 SOLICITATION LETTER must be signed by the GT/ GLT and MKC/ LMKC

Discipline

4.6.1 In the interest of order and maintaining discipline, all officers and members of the Tau Gamma Phi/ Sigma Real Chapter are subject to Chapter Rules and Regulations, Policies, and Guidelines. These include verbal orders and circulars from officers and advisers.
4.6.2 Every Triskelion shall strive to lead a morally upright and virtuous life following the Tenets and Codes of Conduct of the Fraternity/ Sorority.
4.6.3 Every Triskelion shall have the right to express his opinions on matters concerning the Fraternity/ Sorority provided that it is put forth constructively.
4.6.4 Any Triskelion who violates the rules, regulations, Tenets, and Codes of Conduct of the Fraternity/ Sorority, commits an act injurious to the prestige, honor, functions, and stability of the Fraternity/ Sorority, or deliberately or continuously does not participate or attend meetings activities, and projects of the Fraternity/ Sorority will be given a corresponding D.A. (disciplinary paddles/suspension, and expulsion) depending on the gravity of the offense committed.
4.6.5 In serious cases which are subject to suspension, any accused Triskelion must be issued a letter to attend a hearing for verification, validation, and resolution as part of the whole democratic due process.

BE GUIDED BY OUR LAWS (TENETS AND CODES OF CONDUCT)
⦁ Fortitude of strength, voluntary/free will, brotherhood, and sisterhood.
⦁ Any Triskelion who violated the tenets and codes of conduct will be given a chance to defend themselves, to state their defense depending on the circumstance and grievance of what they did before the chapter Officers and the Council. If proven guilty they will be subject to suspension or disciplinary action may declare by the chapter officers and the council.
⦁ Suspended members of the chapter are NOT ALLOWED to stay, attend or participate in any Fraternity/ Sorority meetings and events.
⦁ Any suspended Triskelion member of the chapter is in freeze of their membership until she complies with the suspension period.
⦁ Any suspended Triskelion members of the chapter shall be announced to the council for public reference.
⦁ Any violations of the above-mentioned are subject to disciplinary action depending on the severity of their actions.

First Offense: 6 Traditional Pads + GT/GLT's Pad
Second Offense: 6 Traditional Pads + 3 dynamic Forces
Third Offense: Expulsion
